About Us

Learning Design & EdTech in Language Teaching My work in learning design and educational technology is grounded in extensive experience as a qualified language teacher and teacher trainer, combined with a strong focus on UX-informed digital learning design. Having worked directly in language classrooms, I design flexible, teacher-centred systems that reflect real teaching conditions rather than idealised workflows. I create assessment and learning solutions that adapt to what is actually taught, supporting delivery across online, mobile, print, and classroom-projected contexts. By integrating sound pedagogy with thoughtful digital design, my work streamlines marking, reporting, and administration, reduces teacher workload, and delivers practical, scalable solutions for learners, teachers, and institutions.
