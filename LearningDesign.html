Solution

Design the assessment system around simple, modular templates that teachers can adapt instantly, without technical friction or platform lock‑in. The goal is to give teachers full control over delivery, content, and alignment with pedagogy and future exam pathways.

  • 1. Flexible Delivery Modes
    Build each test so it can be deployed in multiple formats with no extra setup:
    • Mobile Q&A with automatic scoring for quick checks or homework.
    • Phone‑based questions + printable answer sheet for peer‑marking, revision sessions, or low‑tech classrooms.
    • Projected test + student phone responses for whole‑class engagement.
  • 2. Easy Teacher Editing and Localisation
    Ensure every test is editable through plain, simple templates:
    • Teachers can update questions to match what they actually taught.
    • Content can be personalised to their city, students, or current events.
    • Editing should be as easy as typing into a Word document and pasting into the template—no navigating complex paid platforms or restrictive interfaces.
    This keeps assessments relevant, authentic, and responsive to real classroom learning.
  • 3. Consistency With Textbook Pedagogy
    Structure the templates so they naturally follow the sequence, skills focus, and scaffolding of the textbook. This ensures:
    • Smooth integration with lesson flow
    • Reinforcement of target language and skills
    • Predictability for students and teachers
    The tests become an extension of the textbook rather than a separate system.
  • 4. Alignment With High‑Stakes Exam Formats
    Incorporate question types that mirror the tasks students will face in exams like PTE and IELTS:
    • Convert listening passages into Write From Dictation‑style items
    • Include summarising, reordering, gap‑fills, and integrated skills tasks
    • Use distractor patterns and difficulty levels that reflect real exam logic
    This builds familiarity and confidence long before students reach those high‑stakes moments.
  • 5. Significant Time and Cost Savings
    Provide ready‑to‑use test templates that require only quick weekly personalisation. This removes the need for teachers to build assessments from scratch, eliminates constant photocopying, and reduces reliance on expensive subscription platforms. Schools save on paper and printing costs, while teachers reclaim valuable preparation time.
