Problems

New Australian musicans are doing it hard. AI systems are increasingly scraping and reproducing music without compensating the creators; the streaming economy is broken, with artists earning only cents per play and forced to rely on live performance to survive; major platforms like Spotify and YouTube Music act as gatekeepers, rarely surfacing local or emerging acts; and algorithm‑driven recommendations overwhelmingly favour overseas artists, leaving Australia’s music scene underexposed and culturally diminished.

Solution

  • Digital EPs via QR Codes: Artists hand their music directly to fans at gigs or wherever they choose.
  • No Gatekeepers: Listeners access the music instantly—no algorithm or platform interference.
  • Support Local: Fans connect directly with emerging and independent Australian talent.
