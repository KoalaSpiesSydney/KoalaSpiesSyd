Sometimes I find modern design a bit too slick, a bit too clever, and often more about the designer than the user. For example, on/off/pause buttons are frequently difficult to locate and lack any tactile quality. My aim with these designs is to create something nostalgic, intuitive, and genuinely easy to use.

These pieces are still in progress. I’m exploring the idea that creative work doesn’t need to sit entirely online or offline. There’s a growing pull back toward the tactile—analog textures, real buttons—and I’m leaning into that.
